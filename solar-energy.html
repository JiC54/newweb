The duties we perform

  • Design, install and maintain street lights solar systems
  • Design a solar power system based on customer needs and structural conditions
  • Assemble and install the racking, panels and electrical equipment
  • Activate and test the solar panel system
  • Install solar home systems for both domestic and industrial use
  • Install back up systems
  • Solar water pump installation for irrigation scheme or domestic use
  • Solar heaters and gysiers installation
  • Solar Electric Practitioning
  • Solar photovoltaic (PV) fabrication and testing
  • Create or interpret the solar panel installation plans, mapping out the steps of the installation and where all of the components will be installed
  • Inspect, maintain and repair solar panel systems, including the solar collectors, concentrators, batteries, pumps, fans, or support structure.
  • Weatherproof the solar installation in accordance with local safety regulations and the specifications of the manufacturer
  • Consult with clients about the costs and benefits of different solar panel systems
  • Conduct site surveys to determine the best location for solar panels and other equipment
